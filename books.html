Get your ice coolers at the ready ladies, because your summer is about to become a whole lot hotter with these top 5 summer romance reads!

By Jade Mills

15th May 2023


It comes as no surprise that romance is the top genre when it comes to books (we all love, well, love! amiright?) but these reads should take center stage on your TBR list (short for: "to be read" in case you didn't know) this summer! These books are packed with great stories, summer vibes and swoonworthy main characters. We are keeping it *mostly* light hearted with these summer picks, but that's not to say the storylines won't pull you in, because trust me - they will! Grab yourself a refreshing drink and get stuck in with one of these top picks!

No.5 The Summer of Broken Rules by K.L Walther

"The mix of budding romance, competitive hijinks, a close-knit circle, as well as dealing with loss, all make for a satisfying read."

No.4 The Spanish Love Deception by Elena Armas

"A wedding in Spain. The most infuriating man. Three days to convince your family you're actually in love. . . "

No.3 Book Lovers by Emily Henry

"Brimming with witty banter, characters you can't help but fall for and off-the-charts chemistry, BOOK LOVERS is Emily Henry's best novel yet."

No.2 Every Summer After by Carley Fortune

"A sweet story about second chances, and how the future we imagine for ourselves is never quite what it turns out to be."

No.1 The Unhoneymooners by Christina Lauren

"The Unhoneymooners is a heartwarming and hilarious romance perfect for anyone who has ever felt unlucky in love."
